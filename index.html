[Edit] [History] [Watch]

Honkai: Star Rail is a free-to-play turn-based RPG developed and published by HoYoverse. Released on April 26, 2023 for PC, iOS, Android, and PlayStation 5, it is the fourth main entry in the Honkai series.

Players take on the role of the Trailblazer, an amnesiac protagonist who accidentally becomes the host of a Stellaron — a catastrophic cosmic seed — and joins the crew of the Astral Express. Together, they journey across multiple worlds to resolve Stellaron crises and uncover the secrets of the godlike beings known as Aeons.

The game has won the App Store iPhone Game of the Year, Google Play's Best Game, and Best Mobile Game at The Game Awards (2023). By 2026 it surpassed 150 million total downloads.

About the Game

Honkai: Star Rail places players aboard the Astral Express, a legendary train that traverses the cosmos via the Star Rail network — a web of golden tracks laid across the universe by the Aeon of Trailblaze, Akivili. The Express carries its eclectic crew from world to world, resolving Stellaron outbreaks and uncovering the hidden truths behind the Aeons and the universe's philosophical foundations.

The game blends science fiction and fantasy, weaving philosophical themes through its Path system — ideological forces embodied by godlike beings. Characters follow a Path that defines both their abilities and their place in the universe's grand story. With an ever-expanding roster of playable characters and new worlds added regularly, Star Rail continues to grow as one of the most acclaimed games in the gacha genre.

Key Features

  • Turn-based Strategy — Deep combat with Skill Points, Break mechanics, and Action Value systems demanding tactical thinking.
  • Rich Narrative — A story-driven adventure with anime cutscenes, fully voiced dialogue, and expansive world-building across planets and space stations.
  • The Path System — 8 playable Paths define each character's combat role, tied to the universe's philosophical fabric and 18 total Aeons.
  • Gacha / Warp System — Acquire characters and Light Cones through a pity-guaranteed gacha system with limited and standard banners.
  • Endgame Modes — Simulated Universe, Memory of Chaos, Pure Fiction, and Apocalyptic Shadow challenge players to push their rosters to the limit.
