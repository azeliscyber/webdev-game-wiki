Characters

[Edit] [History] [Watch]

Honkai: Star Rail features an ever-growing roster of playable characters, each following one of the eight playable Paths and wielding one of seven elements. Below are two of the game's most prominent factions.

Astral Express

Travellers following the Path of Trailblaze — wanderers who journey across worlds resolving Stellaron crises and uncovering cosmic truths.

Multi-Path

The Trailblazer (You)

Awakened on Herta Space Station, the Trailblazer is the playable protagonist. A Stellaron Host who chooses between Stelle or Caelus — and their Receptacle Codename.

When there is the chance to make a choice, make one that you know you won't regret.
Preservation / Hunt

March 7th

Saved from eternal ice by the Astral Express, she awoke with no memories and named herself after the date of her rebirth. She photographs everything in hopes of recovering her past.

I saved everyone without causing any trouble! You're pretty awesome, March 7th!
The Hunt

Dan Heng

Cold and reserved, he serves as train guard and archivist. Wielding the spear Cloud-Piercer, he joined the crew to escape a painful and secretive past tied to the Xianzhou Alliance.

Farewells are happening throughout the universe even as we speak. The grief we share is real, but there is nothing special about it.
Erudition

Himeko

An adventurous scientist who repaired a stranded Astral Express as a child. She now serves as the train's navigator — always steering the crew toward the next great discovery.

Alright, crew! This world is the target of our next trailblazing expedition!
Nihility

Welt

An animator by trade and former sovereign of Anti-Entropy. Welt has saved Earth from annihilation time and again, carrying the weight of a world — its very name — on his shoulders.

The galaxy is vast beyond compare. An individual's fate should never be limited to a single path ordained by heaven.
Harmony

Sunday

The dream of the Order has dissipated, yet there are still those who will not abandon their original intent — among them, a traveller whose wings were clipped but whose conviction burns on.

There is always a paradise that needs to be built. That vow is like the sun — perhaps I'll melt before reaching it, but some hardships I must endure.

Stellaron Hunters

A band of powerful individuals united under the prophet Elio, following the Script of Finality — walking the Path of Finality to avert the Theory of Four Apocalypses.

Nihility

Kafka

A serene and composed member of the Stellaron Hunters, Kafka serves as Elio's enforcer. She specialises in triggering DoT effects and is closely tied to the Trailblazer's early memories and awakening.

This is all per the script. Don't worry — there won't be any improvisation today.
Nihility

Silver Wolf

A genius hacker who views the universe as one vast simulation to be exploited. She can implant elemental weaknesses into enemies and gleefully bends the rules of reality — digital or otherwise.

I just figured out an exploit. Boss fight? More like a tutorial level now.
Destruction

Blade

An ancient warrior cursed with unkillable immortality, Blade has been seeking death for centuries. He fights by sacrificing his own HP to deal devastating damage — longing for an end that never comes.

I have died a thousand deaths, and I can die a thousand more. What do you have that I haven't already lost?
Destruction

Firefly

A girl from the ruined world of Glamoth, Firefly pilots the mech suit SAM and is revealed to be one of Elio's closest companions. Her warmth conceals a terminal condition and a fierce resolve.

I might not have long, but I'll burn as bright as I can while I'm still here. Isn't that enough?
